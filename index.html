FAQ

Common questions.

Is PrivacyLens free? ▾
Yes — PrivacyLens will always have a free tier covering tracker detection, privacy scores, and plain-English explanations. A Pro tier with advanced features (one-click blocking, tracker history, export reports) will be an optional upgrade.
Does PrivacyLens collect my browsing data? ▾
Absolutely not. This is our core promise. Every bit of analysis runs locally on your device. We have no server that receives your URLs, your habits, or anything about what you visit. Building a privacy tool that violates privacy would be absurd.
How is this different from uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger? ▾
Those are excellent tools — but built for people who already understand tracking. PrivacyLens is built for everyone else. We focus on transparency: not just blocking, but explaining. You'll leave knowing more than when you arrived.
When does PrivacyLens launch? ▾
We're in active development now. Waitlist members get early access before the public Chrome Web Store launch — plus a free Pro trial as a thank-you for joining early.
Will blocking trackers break websites? ▾
PrivacyLens uses smart, surgical blocking — targeting only tracking scripts, not core functionality. In rare cases where blocking might cause issues, we warn you first and make it easy to whitelist a site in one click.
